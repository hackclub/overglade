step one - log in to milkyway, your digital gamedev house.

step two - make games and log hours to earn coins!

step three - purchase a ticket to overglade from the milkyway shop. we'll take care of the rest.

What is Overglade?

Overglade is a 5-day immersive game jam in Singapore, from 29 January 2025 to 2 February 2025.

What is an immersive game jam?

Imagine being in a part of a huge game for five days — where your every move is an action in an alternate universe. You're transported into a magical world. To win the game jam, you have to win the game.

Am I eligible?

Overglade is for teenagers between age 13 to 18!

How do I join?

Make games in Milkyway and purchase an Overglade ticket from the Milkyway shop to come! Food and accommodation is fully covered. You can also purchase flight stipends from the Milkyway shop.

How much does it cost?

Participation in Overglade is entirely free (once you get the ticket through Milkyway)! Food, accommodation, and everything related to your stay is fully covered. You can get flight stipends to cover your flight, too.

Who is running this?

Milkyway is a part of Hack Club, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization.

For more information, join the Hack Club Slack and ask in #overglade. Can't wait to see you there!
